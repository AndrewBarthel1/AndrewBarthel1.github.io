Location

Range: Front

Elevation: 10,750 ft

Cordinates: 39.662958, -105.817198

Road Difficulty

A rating of 4/5 means an unpaved road that is heavy in rocks. There may be steep inclines and ruts that make high clearance nessesary

Nessesary Clearance: 6.5 inches

Other Info

Parking: Pull-offs near river

Campsite Ground Hardness: Rocky and wet. Take time to make sure kickstand is stable as winds are high

Bathrooms: None

Description

Grizzly Gulch is a harder trailhead to access, but grants access to Grey's and Torreys', abslute classics of fourteeners. The road is narrow, giving a nice advantage to traveling with a lower profile. Despite this, the road is dusty and can easily cause skids, so it is recommended to use slim shifting to stay on the road. ABS should be turned off on this road, and caution should be emphasized due to the cliff to the left of the road.
